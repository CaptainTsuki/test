Cell style animation

How anime is made

Cel animation is one of the most traditional forms of animation and involves objects - usually characters - being hand-drawn on clear celluloid sheets and placed over painted backgrounds. These are known as animated cels or animation cels. Nowadays, animators use digital illustration programs instead of physical cells in the animation process.

Stop motion animation

Traditional animation

Stop-motion animation is the oldest form of animation. Photos of characters are taken in sequence and then assembled together and edited into a cohesive video.

3d animation

How disney and pixar's movies are made

3d animation is a style of animation where 3d models made in softwares such as maya or blender are animated with code. To paint in the models, shaders or textures are used.
